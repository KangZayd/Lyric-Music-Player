IUST [show]

The Iran University of Science and Technology  is a research institution
and university of engineering and science in Iran, offering both undergraduate
and postgraduate studies. Located on Hengam Street in the Narmak neighborhood 
in northeast Tehran, IUST and its surrounding communities provide a cultural 
and recreational environment suited to the work of a major research institution.
The university consistently ranks among Iran's top three technical universities.
